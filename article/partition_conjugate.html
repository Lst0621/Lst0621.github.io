Partition Conjugate ($\lambda \to \lambda'$)

Generates 10 random non-negative integers (each < 10), sorts them into a partition $\lambda$, then computes the conjugate partition $\lambda'$ via the standard formula $\lambda'_j = \#\{ i : \lambda_i \ge j \}$.

A: $\lambda$ (rows)
B: $\lambda'$ (cols)
C: $\lambda'$ (rows)
D: $(\lambda')' = \lambda$ (cols)
A$\to$B is conjugation; C$\to$D conjugates back. Numbers label row lengths / column heights.

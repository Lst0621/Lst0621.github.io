Edith "Ditha" Mose's Calendar



This project is based on the 1911 calendar of Dita Moser, an Austrian Viennese Secessionist artist.
Each month is illustrated with images of the twelve main gods of Norse mythology.
The 1st day of each month always appears in the top left corner.
In addition to the original 1911 version, now you can view calendar in this style for any year.
Collaborated with Xicheng Yuan

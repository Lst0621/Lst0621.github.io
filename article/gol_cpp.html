Game of Life (C++ / WASM)

Start
Seed
Wormholes i Adds extra random undirected edges (wormholes) between random pairs of cells. Fixed during a run, re-sampled on Restart. Wormholes can connect even cut nodes.
Cuts i Randomly selects K nodes and removes all 8 base-neighborhood edges for each. Fixed during a run, re-sampled on Restart. Wormholes are applied after cuts.
Scale 4
Topology i Underlying state space is the cartesian product of coordinates. Finite: [0..n) × [0..n). Torus: Zₙ × Zₙ (wrap x and y). Cylinder: Zₙ × [0..n) (wrap x only).
